# --- SIMPLE QUESTIONS ---

Q: What is AI?
A: AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. It refers to computer systems designed to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, like understanding language, recognizing patterns, and making decisions.

Q: What is machine learning?
A: Machine learning is a type of AI where computers learn from data instead of following explicit instructions. The computer finds patterns in examples and uses them to make predictions or decisions.

Q: What is a neural network?
A: A neural network is a computing system inspired by the human brain. It consists of connected nodes (like neurons) that process information in layers, learning to recognize patterns from training data.

Q: How do computers work?
A: Computers process information using electronic circuits that can be in one of two states (on or off, 1 or 0). By combining billions of these simple switches and following instructions (programs), they can perform complex tasks.

Q: What is programming?
A: Programming is writing instructions that tell a computer what to do. Programmers use special languages (like Python, JavaScript, or C++) to create software, apps, games, and AI systems like me.
